> [!IMPORTANT]
> The GPT-4.1 model family (`gpt-4.1`, `gpt-4.1-mini`, `gpt-4.1-nano`) is being retired in October 2026. We recommend migrating to `gpt-5.2`, which provides improved capabilities. For the full retirement schedule, see [Azure OpenAI model retirements](/azure/foundry/openai/concepts/model-retirement-schedule).
